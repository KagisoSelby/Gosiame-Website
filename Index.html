Comprehensive Payroll Solutions

Gosiame Payrolls is a payroll consulting company that specializes in helping businesses outsource their payroll functions. We aim to make payroll easier and more affordable for small and medium-sized enterprises, helping them cut costs while remaining compliant.

Our services range from generating payslips to filing tax documents, and we are committed to growth and expanding into more HR services and Bookkeeping services

Payroll Services

  • Generating payslips
  • Calculating monthly payroll
  • PAYE and UIF submissions
  • Payroll reporting
  • Completing UI-19 forms
  • IRP5/IT3a Recon certificates
  • Leave management HR services

We aim to simplify the payroll process for small and medium-sized businesses, allowing you to focus on your core operations while staying compliant and reducing payroll costs.
